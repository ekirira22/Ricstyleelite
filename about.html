About Us

Welcome to Queenspark Guest House Nyeri, we are located in a homely, serene and secure area
We have elegantly furnished rooms, Bed & Breakfast, restaurant services, 24/7 reception and secure parking.
Queenspark is luxury 4 star hotel, superbly located in the picturesque neighborhood of Outspan Hotel at Baden Powell road roundabout opposite Outspan Hospital. It is 5 km from the City centre, which proves ideal for delegates, Business proffessionals and Tourists. It has a total of 10 rooms of different categories and offers a wide variety of services including conferences and accomodation. For our disabled guests, the main entrance of the hotel has a wheelchair ramp.Disabled rooms are also available with a range of mobility equipments. We pride in our staff whose service will exceed your expectation and we provide the best security to all our guests. Hotel guests touring Nyeri City.

What We Do

We strive to be the best Guest House in Nyeri. And for a remote area with few hotels, we want to offer the best to our local guests, passing by visitors and tourists

We offer a variety of services from conferences, accomodation, food catering services, restaurant services and special services for our guests which even include laundry services

We also happen to be close to Aberdare National Park, and visitors can view game in the only park within the city or visit the Animal Opharnage or snake park which can be arranged on request and an extra charge.
