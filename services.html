Services

  • Accomodation

    We offer accomodation services to Our guests according to their tastes and requirements.

    All room packages come with different pricings which also depend on the number of occupants

    There is free and fast internet in the rooms which also have TV sets for entertainment.

    We also offer Breakfast that is included in the room prices

  • Restaurant services

    Queenspark Guest House is also a hotel that serves Food and Drinks to any customer, not

    necessarily room occupants. During the day Queenspark is an open hotel. We also take orders

    for food and can deliver to customers within a 750m radius

  • Laundry Services

    For Guests that are staying for more than two days. We offer laundry services at a small

    charging fee. We also do the ironing if required.

  • Outside Catering

    Queenspark Guest House is diverse in that it offers outside catering services for outside events and family gatherings
